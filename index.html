About Me

As a web developer, I draw upon my rich background in design and motion, cultivated through years of experience in both design and the dynamic realm of TV and feature film animation. I take pride in showcasing this distinctive fusion of skills at the forefront of my work.

In close collaboration with clients, I am dedicated to translating their vision into an outstanding website. My approach involves seamlessly melding my design acumen with programming expertise to bring their ideas to life, creating a compelling digital presence.

Beyond web development, I extend my capabilities to app development and currently engage in the creation of scripting tools. These tools are designed to automate various tasks related to animation tools, reflecting my commitment to efficiency and innovation in every facet of my work.

Cafe-Boulange Website

Welcome to the Cafe-Boulange website repository! This is a fictional storefront website I designed using Figma and coded using CSS, HTML, and JavaScript. Some anchor tags, such as the social media icons, are left empty as it is a fictional store. I have plans to enhance the website with additional pages and features in the near future.

Design Process

Initiated the design process by curating a selection of images from Pexels. Utilized Figma to intricately design the website layout, ensuring a cohesive and visually appealing interface. Executed the design implementation by employing a combination of CSS, HTML, and JavaScript, bringing the envisioned concept to life on the digital platform.

Sound Design Portfoli

This website was developed for a client's sound design portfolio. The visual design was meticulously crafted using Figma, aligning closely with the client's sound design style. The design was brought to life using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create an atmospheric experience that resonates with the sound designer's work.

Design Process

The visual design was created using Figma, ensuring it matched the client's sound design aesthetics. As part of the ambiance-building strategy, the client contributed images, utilizing their photography hobby, which serve as a key asset in enhancing the overall atmosphere of the website. This design concept was then implemented using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create a captivating web experience that complements the sound designer's work.
